WHAT WE DO


We deal with a wide spectrum of Marine & Industrial Oily waste management services such as Sludge Oil Discharge, Slop Discharge, Bilge Water Discharge, Wash Water Disposal, Vessel Tank Cleaning, Engine room cleaning & De-slopping from vessel under Marine Pollution (MARPOL 73/78) – International Maritime Organization (IMO) Rules & Regulations. Especially our supportive services provide their expertise in the field of Sludge Disposal & Marine Tank Cleaning with firm base in all the major ports in UAE. Moreover, all our activities are approved by the IMO & Environmental Protection Department in UAE.
